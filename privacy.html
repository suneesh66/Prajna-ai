Privacy Policy

Prajna, Inc. (“Prajna,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides agentic AI solutions for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit our website, contact us, request a demo, engage with our team, or use our products and services.

We are an early-stage company, and our privacy practices will continue to mature as our business, products, and compliance obligations grow. We will update this Privacy Policy when our practices materially change.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Information you provide directly:
Name, business email address, phone number, company name, job title, inquiry details, demo requests, sales communications, support requests, and other information you choose to provide.

Business and customer account information:
Information needed to create, manage, support, and administer customer accounts, including authorized users, billing contacts, implementation contacts, and configuration details.

Product and service data:
Depending on the services used, customers may provide business content such as documents, emails, call transcripts, chat messages, workflow data, knowledge-base materials, or other enterprise content for processing by Prajna’s AI systems.

Website and technical information:
IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, pages visited, date and time of visit, and basic usage information collected through cookies, analytics tools, logs, or similar technologies.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

• Operate, provide, improve, and secure our website, products, and services.
• Respond to inquiries, demo requests, and support needs.
• Configure and deliver customer implementations.
• Communicate with prospects, customers, partners, and vendors.
• Analyze website and product usage to improve performance and user experience.
• Maintain security, prevent misuse, troubleshoot issues, and protect our systems.
• Comply with legal, contractual, accounting, and regulatory obligations.
• Develop and improve Prajna’s offerings, subject to applicable customer agreements and data-use restrictions.

3. AI Processing and Customer Content

Prajna’s services may process customer-provided content using AI models, automation workflows, retrieval systems, and related infrastructure.

Customer content is used to provide the contracted service and related support. We do not claim ownership of customer content. Customers are responsible for ensuring they have the right to provide content to Prajna for processing.

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Prajna does not use customer production data to train third-party foundation models. Where third-party AI, cloud, infrastructure, analytics, or support providers are used, they are used to help deliver the service and are expected to process information only for authorized business purposes.
