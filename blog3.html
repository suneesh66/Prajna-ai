April 2024

Prajna joins NVIDIA inception

This partnership will accelerate highly accurate, customized generative AI solutions for the financial services industry, ensuring we can meet the strictest compliance and security standards.

In an exciting development for the financial technology sector, Prajna Inc., a leading artificial intelligence company, has announced its acceptance into the prestigious NVIDIA Inception program. This partnership marks a significant milestone in Prajna's mission to revolutionize the financial services industry through cutting-edge generative AI solutions.

Powering innovation in financial services

The NVIDIA Inception program is renowned for nurturing startups that are pushing the boundaries of AI and data science across various industries. For Prajna, this collaboration opens doors to an array of advanced resources that will accelerate their development of AI solutions tailored for the unique challenges of the financial sector.

Dr. Ramesh Sarukkai, founder and CEO of Prajna, emphasized the importance of this partnership: "Ensuring we can develop, customize and deploy responsible AI solutions that meet the strictest compliance and security standards is paramount in financial services. Joining the NVIDIA Inception program provides immense value, allowing us to leverage cutting-edge hardware and software to push the boundaries of what's possible with generative AI in this highly regulated industry."

Access to cutting-edge technology

As a member of the NVIDIA Inception program, Prajna gains access to:

  • Latest GPU hardware
  • Advanced AI software libraries
  • Technical expertise and support
  • Cloud service support
  • Discounts on NVIDIA hardware
  • Collaboration opportunities with AI experts

These resources will be instrumental in Prajna's efforts to train large language models and accelerate the delivery of cross-channel AI solutions for banks, insurers, and other financial institutions.

Driving ROI through AI applications

Prajna's proprietary platform is designed to help financial enterprises realize significant returns on investment through generative AI applications. Their solutions cater to various aspects of the financial industry, including:

  • Financial lending
  • Sales operations
  • Compliance
  • Customer experience and support

With the boost from NVIDIA Inception, Prajna is poised to enhance its capabilities in developing highly accurate, customized generative AI solutions that can be efficiently deployed within heavily regulated financial environments.

Looking ahead

The partnership between Prajna and NVIDIA Inception represents a convergence of innovative AI technology and deep industry expertise. As Prajna continues to grow and develop its AI solutions, the financial services sector can look forward to more efficient, secure, and compliant AI-driven tools that have the potential to transform the industry.
